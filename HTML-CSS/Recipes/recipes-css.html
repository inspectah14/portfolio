Bucatini all'Amatriciana

Ingredients

  • Bucatini
  • Guenciale
  • Pecorino
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Tomato purè
  • Peperoncini
  • Garlic
  • Yellow onion

Recipe

  1. Fry the guenciale until crisp.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, fry until softened.
  3. Add the tomato purè and the peperoncini and fry for a bit.
  4. Add the cherry tomatoes and let simmer for about 15 min.
  5. Cook the pasta, save about 1dl of the pasta water.
  6. Add the pasta water and 2/3 of the pecorino to the sauce.
  7. Add salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste.
  8. Serve with grated pecorino and some fresh ground black pepper.

Fun Fact!

Amatriciana is one of the classic Roman pasta dishes along with Gricia, Cacio e Pepe, and Carbonara.

Linguine alle Vongole

Ingredients

  • Linguine
  • Clams
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Red chilli
  • Tomato passata
  • White wine
  • Olive oil

Recipe

  1. Sautè the garlic, parsley, and chillies in oil until softened.
  2. Add the passata and white wine.
  3. Add salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste and keep on simmering.
  4. Cook the pasta.
  5. Add the clams and simmer until the clams have opened.
  6. Serve with some fresh parsley on top.

Fun Fact!

'Vongole' means clams in Italian, and thus, any type of clam may be used.

Rigatoni alla Salsiccia

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni
  • Minced salsiccia
  • Garlic
  • Yellow onions
  • Fresh thyme
  • Fresh oregano
  • Fresh parsley
  • Red wine
  • Heavy cream
  • Tomato passata
  • Tomato purè
  • Peperoncini
  • Parmsesan

Recipe

  1. Fry the salsiccia until cooked and almost crisp.
  2. Add the garlic and onions and sautè until softened.
  3. Add the tomato purè and peperoncini and sautè for a bit.
  4. Add the passata, cream, and red wine, all to taste.
  5. Add salt, fresh ground black pepper, and herbs to taste.
  6. Serve with grated parmesan on top.

Fun Fact!

There is minced salsiccia available so you can save time not having to extract the meat from the sausage skin.
