A summary of myself and some of my experiences

As mentioned previously, I have spent many years as a marketer, both as a professional and as a student. Additionally, I have an international background where I graduated from high school in Panamá, did my bachelor's in New York, and my master's here in Stockholm where I currently still reside.

I am a driven and analytical person who is not afraid to pull the right strings to get a task done. Moreover, I am a social and independent team player that speaks three languages fluently: Swedish, English, and Spanish. In my spare time, I very much enjoy sports (both in practice and in front of the TV), hanging out with friends and family, traveling, and also restaurants and wines.

Ultimately, I chose a new career path in frontend development since I love problem-solving and being able to view the results of your code in real-time. It is truly an amazing feeling having worked on the same line of code for hours and then finally getting it right.
